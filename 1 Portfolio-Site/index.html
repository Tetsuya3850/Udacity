Tangible Earth Museum

TANGIBLE EARTH is the world’s first interactive digital globe that dynamically represents various aspects of the earth. It is a powerful tool for researchers, educators, policy makers and the general public to illustrate present conditions of the earth, and to promote a deeper understanding of global challenges in a planetary context.

Earth Solutions Panel

Earth Solutions Panel is a table top interface system which you could interact with your hands to search through various good practices that is creating planetary value (CPV). There are 4 generes: Forest, Water, Food, Disasters. The system is based on Microsoft Kinect.

GAR for Tangible Earth(GfT)

A fully interactive stand-alone free Earth science application for learning about the past several decades in the life of our planet, and the role of the UNISDR. Added features include and ever-increasing body of data about good practices for preparing for and ameliorating disasters when they arise, and real-time disaster alerts.
